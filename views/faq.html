Technology & Implementation

What are the exact specifications for CO2IN?

CO2IN is a Litecoin branch with the following parameters:
  • Addition of DigiSign difficulty re-adjustment
  • Pre-mine of 20% of total currency to here
  • x minute difficulty retarget
  • y initial reward with half life z
  • w target mine time

You say it's a Litecoin branch, but what's the real the lineage of the CO2IN codebase?

CO2IN really is a branch of Litecoin! The most significant changes are a hard-coded pre-mine to certain addresses (see the FAQ section on carbon offset purchase) and the DigiSign difficulty re-adjustment algorithm spliced-in. We will periodically merge the latest updates to Litecoin as development resources allow.

Why didn't you use Proof-of-Stake mining to avoid the energy impact of mining? Why didn't you try to create a community voting system for deciding how to spend the pre-mine on offsets? Why doesn't CO2IN incorporate cool features I've seen in other cryptocurrencies?

The sheer diversity of cryptocurrency implementations is amazing and a testament to the entrepreneurial spirit of the community. Proof-of-stake and voting-based spends are amazing ideas that we hope will be borne out some day, but for us they are too much of a risk. The reality is that the design of a distrbuted system as complex as a cryptocurrency is really, really difficult. We purposefully made CO2IN as small a branch of Litecoin as possible to avoid the introduction of bugs.
